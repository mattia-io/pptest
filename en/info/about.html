About

Precious Plastic is a project trying to boost plastic recycling worldwide. We try to do that by providing tools and knowledge to people around the world. For free, of course. Trying to give people solutions to fight plastic pollution.

Precious Plastic was started by Dave Hakkens in 2013. By now, the project counts on the contributions of dozens of people joining the project with their skills and knowledge. The project is also helped by hundreds of monthly donations on our Patreon and thousands single donations. Check the video below for the full story behind Precious Plastic.

The project has been picked up by hundreds of people around the world that built the machines and started recycling plastic waste. Check our map to get a feeling.
