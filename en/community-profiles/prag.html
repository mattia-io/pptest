Prag

Hi this is Prag Design studio from Seoul, South Korea concerned with producing goods and product design while trying to find sustainable value within manufacturing processes. Prag's ultimate goal is to make an interactive-small-mobile-factory, Desk Factory, to create workshops across Seoul and educate people about plastic recycling.

Desk Factory

Well, let me say, Prag's Desk Factory is brilliant- we absolutely love it. A well designed, compact, beautiful and fully functional mobile recycling factory to inspire people in Korea. Prag has already made two version of the Desk Factory, one to produce lamps and a second one to make toys. Fantastic!

Products

Currently Prag is not focusing particularly on product but rather trying to optimize the Desk Factory and create ever more engaging and informative workshops around town. This being said we still love their eccentric lamps and cute vases.
