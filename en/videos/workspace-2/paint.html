Paint the container

Last but not least it is time to paint the container and work on the branding so people will know what you're working on. In this video we'll go through best practices, tips and tricks on painting such a massive surface and methods to brand your container that are easy and affordable. This step can seem trivial but it is important to create a nice, clean and pleasing workspace for you and your customers.

Paint with friends

Painting such a massive surface can be daunting and very time consuming- oh, and rather boring after the second day. We like to invite friends over so they can help us out and we can have an excuse to catch up :)

Draw the logo

Precious Plastic logo was originally designed in an open and collaborative manner with the community. The logo is handwritten which makes it easier to replicate by hand by anyone around the world. Well, unless you're an artist, that's easier said than done. To make it easier on ourselves we like to use a projector in the process so we can simply trace over the projected logo. Alternatively you could use stencils.
