Become a machine builder

In this video we will show you some tips and tricks to streamline your building process so you can start to make machines and parts for people around you. Dozens of people contact us every month to buy the machines around the world, but we don't really want to mass produce the machines and ship them around the world as we don't see this as very sustainable in the long run. Instead, we envision a network of many machine builders around the world making machines, parts and component for people that wants them in their region. Less delivery miles and someone nearby if something needs repair.

Pro tip: if you're considering becoming a machine builder it makes sense to build multiple machines or parts at the same time to cut costs, time and delivery.
